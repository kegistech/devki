      Reviving Idar’s Toy-Making Heritage with Modern Innovation.

      Devki Exim is a dynamic retail business in the eCommerce toys and kids’ products industry, proudly rooted in the culturally rich town of Idar, Sabarkantha — historically celebrated as a thriving hub of traditional toy-making craftsmanship.

      Every product offered under Devki Exim’s carefully curated toy brands undergoes strict quality control and safety testing to meet modern industry standards. This commitment guarantees durability, child-safe materials, and long-lasting play value — giving parents complete confidence in every purchase.
